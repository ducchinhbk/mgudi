Motivated, enthusiastic, dedicated and professional – what more could an employer ask for? But Graduate recruitment marketing is a challenging and competitive venture. Over the years Wave have developed a reputation for delivering graduate attraction schemes that are award winning and results driven.

OUR PROCESS

Understanding

Knowledge is power as they say, so our starting point
is knowing who you want to attract and why.

  • We need to know why Grads should be attracted to you, what makes you better compared to your competitors.
  • What's hot, what's not, best for us to know the full picture in order to build a campaign for you to attract the audience you need to.

Objective

Objective

So, what are you trying to achieve? Any campaign that's running should have an objective but that can take many different forms.

  • Ultimately you want to hire the best talent you can, but there will be several objectives to meet before that.
  • From attracting footfall, increased traffic to your career site, applications, enquiries, engagement, even improvements to your Graduate recruitment league ranking.
  • We'll map out what's needed, the key milestone that we need to reach.

Solution

Solution

Reaching your graduate audience can take many forms. Depending on who, where and how, your solution can vary.

  • We're full service, meaning we get involved in everything from building recruitment websites, University open days, brochure development and film production.
  • Some campaigns are comprehensive, others simple and straight-forward. All comes down to what you're trying to achieve.

Results

Results

Did it work? With all our campaigns, we track, tag and record everything.

  • We'll report back on sessions, bounce rates, applications, engagement and reach.
  • All put together in a summary that will highlight what worked and what needs improving for next time.
