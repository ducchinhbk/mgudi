Program Syllabus

15 Modules20 weeks

Introduction to Product Management

You will start the program with a basic understanding of product management and the key skills required to become a successful product manager. On completion of the first module, you will have an understanding of what the expectations from a product manager are, how they vary across companies and across industries, and what it takes to be a successful product manager.

Introduction to User Research

To build products that users would love, you first need to find out what they want. This module will help you understand how you can get to know your users through common methods of user research like interview, survey and contextual inquiry. You will also learn how to analyse the data you collect and how to gather insights from it that will help you develop the product.

Building Artifacts from User Research

As a PM, you will be the voice of the user to the product’s stakeholders. To get everyone on the same level of understanding, you will need artifacts that can convey the user’s world view. Personas, user journey maps and user stories are some of the things you will learn to create from your user research.

User-Centered Design

Many great products fail due to bad design. A product solving all the pain points of the user will still need to be perfectly designed so that the user can actually use it. You will understand how to build products with design that keeps the user in focus. This module will also cover new standards in product design.

Prototyping

Before investing time and resources on your product, it is better to build prototypes, so that you can test them on actual users to get feedback. You will learn how to create paper prototypes, wireframes (using tools like Balsamiq, Proto.io) and high fidelity prototypes like mockups (using Invision).

Design Validation

Now that you have a prototype, you need to be aware of how to capture user feedback in an organised manner to improve the design. Through these modules, you will learn various user and data-based design validation methods like usability testing and minimum viable product.

Product Planning and Development

Taking a product to development requires the PM to play a critical role in deciding the priority of the features that should go into development, defining the requirements with clarity, and working with tech and design teams during the sprint. Through these modules, you will learn about the fundamentals of scrum product development, sprint planning, writing effective product requirements and creating a product roadmap.

Product Deployment

The product is ready to ship. The focus now is for the feature to perform in a stable manner. In this module, you will learn about the application of CI/CD (continuous integration and continuous deployment) and DevOps.

Product Analytics

Managing a product requires the product manager to gauge the performance of the product on metrics. In this module, you will learn the metrics that need to be tracked, methods to track them and how to interpret the data.

User Engagement and Growth

When the product is live, your main focus will be to increase the engagement of the users across the product and develop hacks to grow the number of users. In this module, you will learn about the various facets of user engagement, and methods to achieve user growth.

Effectiveness

The success of a PM is dependent on more than strength in hard skills alone. The primary indicator of success is her strength in leading without authority. Through this module, you will learn how to lead without authority, prioritise, and get more done with less.

Other topics

In this module, you will get exposure to other topics that you might be expected to know based on the type of products or features that you will manage. APIs, payments and go to market are some of the topics covered in this module.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the time commitment expected for this product management certification program?

Devoting 6-8 hours per week will be enough to get you through the program. The more time you devote, the greater will be the learning you gain from the program.

Q2. Is there any certification granted at the end of the product management program?

Yes, post completion of this online product management program, a personalised certificate would be issued by UpGrad with your performance across key product management competencies and skills mapped.

Q3. Is there any minimum educational qualification required to take this product management certification program?

The applicant should have at least one year of work experience and an undergraduate degree. A background in technology is preferred.

Q4. What is the selection process for this online product management course?

Renowned faculty and many industry leaders have committed a lot of time in conceptualising and creating this online product management course to make sure that the candidates can receive the best possible learning experience in product management.

Hence, we want to make sure that the participants of this product management course also show a very high level of commitment and passion towards product management.

We have a two-tiered selection process where the applicants first need to fill an admission form (asking for work and educational details, followed by a short Q&A to gauge product understanding). For the shortlisted candidates, this will be followed by a short 15-minute telephonic conversation with our product management program team.

Q5. What kind of projects will I be expected to work on?

Providing real-life industry exposure will be one of the key focus areas for the course. There will be various projects/case studies to help you understand each module. An example of a project you might get is:

'You have joined as a PM at Furlenco. At the end of two weeks, you have to present an understanding of the typical user of Furlenco and submit a usability audit of web and mobile app to the product head with suggested improvements. Perform user research and usability audit and submit the powerpoint that you will use for the presentation.'

Q6. What kind of placement assistance will be provided by UpGrad?

A product portfolio/experience of working with products is perhaps the most important component, keenly looked for, by most product companies while hiring PMs.

With UpGrad, you will be working on various projects/case studies (refer to question 5) that will help you build your product portfolio. UpGrad will directly forward these projects to relevant leading companies in the industry. This will help you get ahead in their candidate screening process.

Based on your performance, UpGrad will also strive to get you interviews at top product firms.

Additionally, dedicated assistance in terms of resume preparation, reviews and mock interviews will be provided.

Q7. Are there networking opportunities? If so, of what nature?

Plenty of networking opportunities. Firstly, we map a participant to an industry mentor.

These are from our mentor network of product heads from leading companies. The mentorship model is 2 half hour sessions per month over 5 months. Also, there will be regular meet ups in Mumbai, Bangalore and Delhi.

These will be led by industry partners. If a participant looking for a particular company/particular contact, we can work towards exploring that.

Q8. Is there any scholarship for the course?

There is no scholarship as such. However, we have a lot of flexible payment options for you to explore. You might want to get on a call with us to know more about the various payment options and offers that we have for you.

Regarding payment, you can either -

  • Make an upfront payment of the entire fee amount
  • Explore one of our many EMI, loan and other flexible payment options
  • Book your seat by paying a non-refundable blocker amount of Rs. 25,000. You can chose to complete the payment at your pace(within a window of 15 days) The blocker amount will reserve your seat in our class.

Q9. Is there any refund policy?

We have a refund policy of 14 days. - If, within the initial 14 days of the start of the program, you are not satisfied, we will refund your payment (after deduction of applicable processing and admin charges) back to you!

Both scholarships for the most talented candidates as well as EMI and loan options are available for flexible payment solutions.
