School Math

Covers the entire curriculum of the CBSE, ICSE and State Boards. Also draws on the US Common Core, UK National and Singapore National Curricula.

Delivered through specially created workbooks that employ the active learning methodology.

At the end of each topic, there will be a tab-based assessment which helps guide the child's further learning.

Mental Aptitude

Builds mathematical thinking by improving verbal, non-verbal, logical and calculation skills.

Structured as a series of game-like tab based exercises that students have to master.

Mastering each skill requires increasing level of accuracy and proficiency.

Creative Reasoning

Develops out-of-the-box thinking through mathematical puzzles. Helps build connections between different domains of math and real life.

At the end of each session, a card with a puzzle on it is assigned to your child.

He/She will be encouraged to work on it collaboratively with friends, parents and siblings.

FAQs

Still not convinced? Wish to know more
about the Cuemath program? Please go through the frequently asked questions.

chat_bubble
What is the duration of the program?

The Cuemath program runs through the year and caters to students in every grade from K through 8. Each Cuemath student attends three 1-hour sessions a week. But given the flexibility of our program, it adapts itself to the student's pace rather than moulding the student to fit its predetermined boundaries.

chat_bubble
How many students are there in a Cuemath session?

There are at most 8 students in a group. It is our wish to ensure that every one of our students is able to ask for and get "personal attention".

chat_bubble
Does Cuemath provide any assessments to the students?

Yes, periodic assessments are part of the program. However, the intention of these assessments is not simply to "pass" or "fail" students, but rather to give the teacher enough information to help guide the student's learning. Additionally, all progress reports are shared with the parents who are encouraged to approach the teacher with their questions or suggestions.

chat_bubble
How is Cuemath different from home based tuitions?

We wish to emphasize that Cuemath is not a tuition. Instead, it is an (after-school) math-learning programme. In consequence, while home-based tuitions focus primarily on the school syllabus (or syllabi) and preparing students for their tests and exams, Cuemath's eye is fixed firmly on the long-term holistic development of a child's mathematical ability.
