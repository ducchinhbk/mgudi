54th Australian Export Awards

The 54th Australian Export Awards ceremony was held in Brisbane on 24th November 2016 before an audience of 500 guests.

Hosted by the Hon. Steven Ciobo MP, Minister for Trade, Tourism and Investment, 12 national category winners were announced at the ceremony with Envato recognised as Australia's Australian Exporter of the Year for 2016.
